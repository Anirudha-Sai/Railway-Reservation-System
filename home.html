About the Railway Reservation System

Our railway reservation system allows you to effortlessly search for available trains, book tickets, check ticket status, and manage your bookings. Whether you're planning a single journey or managing reservations for a group, our system simplifies the entire process.

Main Features

Search Trains

Find available trains between different cities with real-time seat availability and fare details.

Book Tickets

Book your tickets seamlessly and securely with multiple payment options and instant booking confirmation.

Ticket Status

Check the status of your ticket, including confirmation status and seat assignment.

Cancel Tickets

Easily cancel your tickets and receive quick refunds based on cancellation policies.
